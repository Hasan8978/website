Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about APEX Atelier memberships, classes, and facilities.

What is included in the 7-day trial?

Our 7-day trial grants full access to all gym facilities, unlimited group classes, and one complimentary consultation with a fitness specialist. No commitment required. You'll experience everything our luxury facility has to offer before making a decision. The trial includes access to our signature recovery lounge and one body composition scan.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my membership?

Yes, members may adjust their tier at any time with 30 days' notice. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply to the next billing cycle. There are no fees for changing your membership level. Platinum members enjoy additional flexibility with month-to-month commitments.

What is the cancellation policy?

Memberships may be canceled anytime with a 30-day written notice. No long-term contracts — only month-to-month flexibility. Email your cancellation request to members@apexatelier.com. Annual members receive a prorated refund for unused months upon cancellation.

Do you offer family or corporate memberships?

Absolutely. Corporate wellness programs and family add-ons are available. Contact our concierge for bespoke group pricing tailored to your organization's needs. Corporate members receive additional perks including on-site wellness workshops and discounted guest passes for employees.

Is there parking available?

Yes, valet parking is available for members and guests at a discounted rate of $15 for up to 3 hours. Self-parking is also available at adjacent garages with validation. Bicycle storage is complimentary.

What is the dress code?

Premium athletic attire is required. We provide complimentary towels, lockers, and amenities including Malin+Goetz products. No jeans or street shoes on training floors. Proper gym etiquette is expected at all times. Closed-toe athletic shoes are required in all training areas.

Can I bring a guest?

All membership tiers include guest passes. Signature members receive 2 per month, Executive members receive 4, and Platinum members receive 8. Additional guest passes may be purchased for $50 per day. Guests must sign a waiver and be accompanied by the member at all times.
